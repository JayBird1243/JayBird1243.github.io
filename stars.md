Starry Sky

The starry background of the site is a procedurally generated piece of mathematical art. The randomly generated 32 digit hexadecimal seed at the top of your screen deterministically generates the visuals.


Field of Stars

The size, position and volume of stars are generated using the first 8 hex digits. 

A pseudo-random number generating algorithm called Mulberry32, which will deterministically generate a string of numbers, ensuring the same seed will always produce the same background
